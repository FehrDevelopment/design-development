Introduction:

Hello, my name is Terence Fehr. Earning my full‑stack diploma with honors has been a long‑time dream, and achieving it has been one of the most meaningful milestones in my life. Over the past year, I’ve immersed myself in both front‑end and back‑end development—pushing boundaries, overcoming challenges, and growing through every step of the journey. I’m a hands‑on learner who thrives on discovering new concepts and putting them into practice. With each project, I gain more confidence, skill, and passion for the craft. I’m currently based in Edmonton, Alberta.

Throughout my studies, I’ve built strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, .NET, Git/GitHub, ASP.NET, and SQL. I’ve also completed coursework in Business Communication, IT fundamentals, Java programming, JavaScript, Testing and Quality Assurance, Web Development, Version Control, Inheritance and Polymorphism, Front‑End Frameworks, Relational Databases, and Object‑Oriented Programming. These experiences have given me a solid foundation in both development and collaborative workflows. I created this website to connect with like‑minded individuals and businesses who want to elevate their online presence or build a fully customized, functional website from the ground up.

I’m especially passionate about building seamless, user‑friendly web applications and helping people bring their ideas to life. I love creating clean, organized, and visually appealing digital experiences—and I always aim to have fun throughout the process. I’m continuously learning new techniques to write better code and refine my craft.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy spending time with my loved ones, practicing mindfulness, and taking life one day at a time. I also love being outdoors—whether I’m skating, relaxing in nature, or spending time with friends. Nature is my escape; it helps me reset, refocus, and recharge for the work ahead.

I’m always open to connecting with like‑minded professionals. Feel free to reach out at terence.fehr@gmail.com or connect with me on LinkedIn.
